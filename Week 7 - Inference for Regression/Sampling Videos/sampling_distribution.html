Sampling

We may be interested in the true relationship between average beauty and course evaluation scores for all of the faculty at the university of Texas at Austin.

  • We take a sample of individuals, with the interest in inferring what we found in our sample to a larger population of individuals.

  • We typically only get one sample, but repeated sampling gives us a better idea of the variation in responses.
